This website is about The Use of IT in medical field.

As there is a general saying, ‘Good is always followed by an evil’ which implies that every sort of things will have its own advantages and at the same time, disadvantages too. Use of ICT in medical can have both pros and cons, but website will just deal with the advancement in ICT which has positively impacted the medical education and medical health care. Use of electronic medical records, initiation of e-health and use of multimedia and internet in medical education are proved to be boon of ICT in medicine and medical education.
